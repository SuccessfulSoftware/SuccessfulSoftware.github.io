SPEI

SPEI is a system developed and operated by Banco de México that allows the general public, in a matter of seconds, to make electronic payments, also called electronic transfers, via a bank, the Internet, or mobile banking. This system allows money to be transferred electronically between bank deposit accounts almost instantly.

SWIFT

SWIFT’s messaging services are used and trusted by more than 11,000 financial institutions in more than 200 countries and territories around the world.

Ripple

Ripple’s distributed financial technology enables banks to send real-time international payments across networks. Using Ripple, banks can meet growing demands for faster, low-cost, on-demand global payment services for any payment size. The result: new revenue opportunities, lower processing costs, and better overall customer experiences.
